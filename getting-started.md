# Getting Started

## Installation

1. Open the [Google Workspace Marketplace](https://workspace.google.com/marketplace/app/grade_reports/167772122975){:target="_blank"}
2. Click **Install** and follow the prompts
3. Grant the required permissions when asked

{: .note }
> Grade Reports requires permissions for Google Classroom, Google Drive, Google Docs, and Gmail. These are used only to read your class data and send reports.

## First Run

1. Open any **Google Doc** (Grade Reports runs as a Google Docs Add-on)
2. Go to **Extensions → Grade Reports → Generate Reports**
3. On first launch, Grade Reports will set up a configuration folder in your Google Drive called **Grade Reports** containing a config spreadsheet

## Setting Up Student Information

Before generating reports, you need to enter student details (names, email addresses, and guardian emails):

1. After selecting a course, click **Enter Student Information**
2. Fill in student names, student emails, and guardian emails
3. Save — this information is stored in your config spreadsheet

Alternatively, click **Open Config Spreadsheet** to edit the spreadsheet directly.

{: .important }
> Guardian emails are required if you want to send reports to parents/guardians. Students can receive reports using their Google Classroom email.

## Generating Your First Report

1. Select a course from the **Google Classroom Course** dropdown
2. Wait for students to load
3. Select the students you want to include (or click **Select All**)
4. Configure your [report options](features/generate-reports.md)
5. Click **Create Report** to generate reports as Google Docs, or **Email Reports** to generate and send them immediately

Reports are saved to a **Grade Reports** folder in your Google Drive, organized by course.
