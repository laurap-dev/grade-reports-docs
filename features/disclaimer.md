# Overall Grade Disclaimer

When you enable **Include overall grade** in report settings, the following terms apply.

---

## Terms of Service for Displaying Overall Grade

By choosing to display overall grades in reports, you acknowledge and agree to the following:

- We strive to calculate overall grades as accurately as possible; however, **grades shown in reports may differ from those displayed in Google Classroom**.
- Google does not provide direct access to the official overall grade via its API. The grade is generated using available data — if Google allowed direct access, this disclaimer would not be necessary.
- Differences may arise due to factors such as draft grades, missing or late assignments, varying grading policies, or incomplete data.
- Calculations may not account for teacher-specific grading customizations or recent changes not yet reflected in synced data.
- **The displayed grade should be considered an estimate and not an official record.**

---

## Recommendation

It is advised that you **do not allow students to view their overall grade in Google Classroom** by adjusting this setting within Google Classroom:

1. Go to [classroom.google.com](https://classroom.google.com)
2. Click your class → **Settings**
3. Under **Grade calculation**, disable **Display overall grade to students**

{: .warning }
> Sharing an estimated grade as an official grade may cause confusion. Always communicate to students and guardians that the overall grade shown is a calculated estimate.
